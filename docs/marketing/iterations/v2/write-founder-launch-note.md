# Founder's Note: Stop practicing LeetCode. Start practicing the interview.

I've spent hundreds of hours grinding LeetCode. It helped me pass the coding screen, but I still felt unprepared for the real thing.

The traditional "Big Tech" interview isn't just about solving a problem. It's about demonstrating your thought process, communicating clearly under pressure, and collaborating with your interviewer.

That's why I built this. It's not another problem-grinding platform. It's a tool to help you practice the *entire* interview, from the initial prompt to the final solution, with an AI companion that gives you real-time feedback.

**The Problem with LeetCode-Style Practice**

*   **No Communication Practice:** You're just coding in silence.
*   **No Pressure Simulation:** It doesn't feel like a real interview.
*   **No Feedback Loop:** You don't know what you're doing wrong.

**How We're Different**

Our platform uses AI to simulate a real interview.

*   **Talk Through Your Solution:** Practice articulating your approach out loud.
*   **Get Real-Time Feedback:** The AI will ask clarifying questions and provide hints, just like a real interviewer.
*   **Analyze Your Performance:** After each session, get a detailed breakdown of your communication, problem-solving, and coding skills.
